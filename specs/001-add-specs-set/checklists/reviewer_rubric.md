# Reviewer Rubric

This rubric maps reviewer guidance to SPEC-REVIEWER-CHECKLIST-001.

- Intentionality: Does the chapter demonstrate clear learning objectives and outcomes?
- Structural compliance: Are five tabs present and correctly labeled?
- Explainability: Are technical choices explained with cause→effect?
- Academic seriousness: Is the level appropriate for senior undergrad/early grad?
- Reproducibility: Are examples actionable with referenced tools and commands?

## Scoring

Each criterion is scored 0–5:
- 0: Not present / unacceptable
- 3: Adequate
- 5: Excellent — clear, actionable, and reproducible

## Rubric items

- Intentionality: Are learning objectives specific and measurable? (0–5)
- Structural compliance: Five tabs present and correctly labeled? (0–5)
- Explainability: Are technical decisions explained with cause→effect and trade-offs? (0–5)
- Academic seriousness: Is the level appropriate for senior undergrad/early grad? (0–5)
- Reproducibility: Examples include commands, configs, or steps referencing tools like ROS 2, Gazebo, Isaac? (0–5)

## Guidance for reviewers

- Prefer constructive feedback: point to section/line numbers and suggest fixes.
- Use the checklist files to record PASS/FAIL per-rule; use this rubric for subjective scoring.
- When in doubt, mark as 'Needs revision' rather than 'Fail' for subjective items.
